About Hash chatbot project

This project involves developing a Hash chatbot in collaboration with other partners. Its purpose is to predict STD diseases based on the symptoms provided by the user in response to chatbot questions. Subsequently, the chatbot will display the most probable disease to the user. It is important to note that the chatbot ensures anonymity and prioritizes the protection of user privacy. Additionally, the project includes recommending a health center near AASTU by utilizing Google Maps.

General Awareness on STDs Protection/Prevention

Sexually transmitted diseases (STDs) are a significant public health concern, and it is essential to promote awareness and education about their prevention and protection.

Get regular testing:-Even if you don't have symptoms, regular STD testing is important for early detection and treatment.

Seek resources and support: Access reliable sources for information, testing services, and support organizations that can provide guidance and counseling.

  • Reduce risk
  • Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis (PrEP)
  • Inform sexual partners
  • Vaccinations
